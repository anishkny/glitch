Email Magic!

New User

Context.IO provides an OAUTH signup flow for Google, Yahoo, and Microsoft email accounts.

Most generic IMAP providers are also supported but for this app just stick to OAUTH.

New Webhook

A webhook is a rule-based notification sent to your app by Context.IO

There are many different ways to use webhooks, but a simple case is to just filter by the email sender.

Select one of your users, and then enter an email address you would like to get notifications about.

Webhook Counts

This app only counts how many webhooks it has recieved for each user.

There are many more uses for webhooks - go crazy!
